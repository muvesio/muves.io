Meet the Team

Dmitry Kan

CEO

Dmitry is a seasoned expert in AI-driven search technologies and product leadership. He holds a PhD in Computer Science and has led search at AlphaSense, served as Principal AI Scientist at Silo AI, and worked as a Product Manager for Search at TomTom. He also hosts the Vector Podcast, sharing insights from the frontier of machine learning.

Aarne Talman

CTO

Aarne brings deep experience in applied NLP and enterprise AI. He holds a PhD in Natural Language Processing and has led ML initiatives across Accenture. He served as CTO at Nordcloud (acquired by IBM) and as Head of Tech at SiloGen (acquired by AMD), combining hands-on technical leadership with strategic vision for building AI platforms.

Stergios Chatzikyriakidis

Head of Research

Stergios is a professor of computational linguistics with a PhD in Linguistics. He has led research at the University of Crete and CLASP in Gothenburg, where he served as Associate Director. His work bridges formal linguistics and machine learning approaches, advancing the intersection of formal semantics and neural language modeling.
