Meet the Team

Dmitry Kan

CEO

Dmitry is a seasoned expert in AI-driven search technologies and product leadership. With a PhD in Computer Science, he has held senior roles including Head of Search at AlphaSense and Principal AI Scientist at Silo AI. He also hosts the Vector Podcast, sharing insights from the frontier of machine learning.

Aarne Talman

CTO

Aarne brings deep experience in applied NLP and enterprise AI. He holds a PhD in Natural Language Processing and has led ML initiatives across Accenture, Nordcloud (acquired by IBM), and SiloGen (acquired by AMD). Aarne combines hands-on technical leadership with strategic vision for building AI platforms.

Stergios Chatzikyriakidis

Head of Research

Stergios is a professor and researcher in computational linguistics with a PhD in Linguistics. He has led research teams at the University of Crete and CLASP in Gothenburg, where he served as Associate Director. His academic focus is on formal semantics, syntax, and statistical language modeling.
