Why Muves?

With the volume of AI research growing almost exponentially, staying current with meaningful developments is a constant challenge—even for experts. Muves helps researchers, students, and technical professionals manage this complexity by organizing research into focused projects. With AI-assisted literature reviews and conversational access to your project collection, Muves keeps you informed and ready to act—without the overwhelm.

Discover Trends

Stay ahead of the curve by following emerging AI research. Muves highlights trending papers and key developments so you can track breakthroughs without the noise.

Save and Organize

Organize research papers by project and keep everything in one place. The Research Assistant agent helps generate literature reviews and lets you chat with your project collection.

Understand Faster

Use AI to break down complex research into clear summaries. Export citations, spot key insights, and let your Research Assistant help you make sense of your readings faster.
